scene iv ephesu a room in cerimon s house enter cerimon and thaisa cerimon madam this letter and some certain jewel lay with you in your coffer which are now at your command know you the character thaisa it is my lord s that i was shipp d at sea i well remember even on my eane time but whether there deliver d by the holy gods i cannot rightly say but since king pericle my wedded lord i ne er shall see again a vest livery will i take me to and never more have joy cerimon madam if this you purpose as ye speak diana s temple is not distant far where you may abide till your date expire moreover if you please a niece of mine shall there attend you thaisa my recompense is thanks that s all yet my good will is great though the gift small exeunt
