were my husband i should freely rejoice in that absence wherein he won honour than in the embrace of his bed where he would show most love when yet he was but tender body and the only son of my womb when youth with comely pluck all gaze his way when for a day of kings entreaty a mother should not sell him an hour from her behold i considering how honour would become such a person that it was no
